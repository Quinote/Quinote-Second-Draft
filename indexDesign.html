Quinote is a Google Drive add-on that serves as a study aid by will reading your notes and generating multiple choice, fill in the blank, and true/false quizzes!

Getting Started:

In order to use Quinote:
  • You must have a Google Email account
  • When you click the Get Quinote button, the app will ask for permission to access your Google Drive
  • After granting permission, you can open documents in your Google Drive with Quinote or create documents with Quinote

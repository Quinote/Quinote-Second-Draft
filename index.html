QuiNote is a study aid that reads your notes and generates quizzes!

A notebook with built-in study aids

Most people take notes. Most people need to review the notes to memorize their content. QuiNote allows you to effortlessly review your notes by quizzing you from them.

QuiNote can generate any combination of true/false, multiple choice, or fill-in-the blank question from one or more note files. Check out our about page to learn how QuiNote works.

Features

Paperless

QuiNote is a web-based application. You can take notes, generate quizzes, and save your progress without printing a single sheet of paper or handwriting any flash cards.

Accessible

All you need is an Internet connection to use QuiNote. There is no software to download and our product is not operating system dependent! Just login and get started.

Customizable

With QuiNote you can generate a multitude of quizzes from one or more sets of notes. Additionally, you can select the type of quiz (true/false, multiple choice, fill-in-the-blank) or choose a combination of the three.

Free

QuiNote was created as a project for a collaborative software development course. We made this program because we thought it would be useful and, as college students, we know useful things should be free.
